The enchanting world of books!

Welcome to my bookshelf...

Books are indeed a necessity more than a hobby I believe. They pave a way for us into the unknown paradise or a world of our imagination. Books takes us places and enable us to live there and escape the reality that surrounds us. Sometime they change the mere and abstract concept of what is reality for us. They make us believe in many worldly as well as imaginative aspects of our existence and life. The life which has so much to show us could also be explored in the bookshelf or in the world of books. They set us free and let our thoughts meet the words and sets wings to our imaginative sea of possibilities.

The world seems to be in our hands when we delve inside of the books. So, it's never too late to start anything and it's never the case with reading a book. So grab a great book today or a pdf version of a book to start and trust me, every moment of it will be worth your precious time. Many great books That I've read and would enjoy reading them again and again are listed below. Do check them out when you decide to take a peek into the fascinating existence of a world that wraps you into the charisma of words.

  • The Alchemist: A really movtivating book that grips you and urges you to explore the beautiful adventure of life with a gripping story.
  • The Kite runner: A must read book that takes you to Afghanistan to a story of a young boy brimming with many emotions and incidents that surround it.
  • Looking for Alaska: Another great book that enfolds a story of a teenager and the quest for understanding love along the way written in beautiful words.
